How it works: The Fibonacci clock uses 5 squares with Fibonacci sizes (1,1,2,3,5) to represent time. Red squares represent hours, Green squares represent minutes (multiplied by 5), Blue squares represent both, and Gold squares are neutral and don't represent any time component. Minutes are rounded to the nearest 5-minute interval.
